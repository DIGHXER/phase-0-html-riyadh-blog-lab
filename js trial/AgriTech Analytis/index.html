How our solution works

1. Starts with agriTech01

This is our solar-powered IoT sensor. It helps early detection of crop pests and diseases as well as capture soil health parameters. It takes pictures and scans of plants, analyses the imagery against a database and reports any disease or pest detected. It also analyses the soil health by capturing soil parameters (soil pH, moisture, temperature, NPK)

2. Issuance of agriTech01 to farmers

Farmers install the sensor in their farms as we guide them. The farmers then downloads our mobile application, registers to receive phone texts and creates an online platform. Once the easy registration process is complete, the farmer is ready to manage his/her farm remotely using their phone or computer.

3. agriTech01 detects crop diseases and pests

The sensor takes pictures and scans of plants’ leaves and stems. It analyses the imagery against a database and reports any disease or pest detected. It also captures crop intensity in order to detect weeds growth and their types. Finally, it reports to the farmers via their mobile phones or computers as an SMS or email.

4. AgriTech01 collects soil parameters

It captures soil parameters; soil NPK, moisture, temperature, pH. The sensor captures the parameters on the soil service and root-deep service. These soil health parameters help to determine amount of water, fertilizers and pestcides to be applied. It gives better crops, uses fewer inputs, and increases profitability.

5. Combine satellite data with agriTech01

This is our magic. This combination helps give over 95% accurate alerts, predictions and reports to farmers. Our ML satellites captures crop pests, crop diseases, crop weeds and soil health parameters just like our sensor. We combine the data between the satellite imagery data and agriTech01 data to produce precise and accurate data sent to farmers. Our satellite data dates back to 2008 and this is again magical. Though the satellite, we are able to give farmers 21 days of accurate weather forecast.
